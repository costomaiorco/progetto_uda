Our Story

RE-TECH started as a school project. The three of us were looking for a topic for our technology class and stumbled upon a report about e-waste in developing countries. What we read shocked us.

We decided we did not want to just write a report about it. We wanted to actually do something. So we created RE-TECH: a company focused on collecting, recycling and giving a second life to discarded electronic devices.

We are still students, we are still learning, but we believe that age is not an excuse to ignore a problem this big.

● Transparency

We publish every month how many kilograms of e-waste we have collected and recycled. No tricks.

● Education

We go to schools and companies to explain the e-waste problem because change starts with awareness.

● Circular Economy

Every device we collect either gets a second life or has its materials safely extracted and reused.
